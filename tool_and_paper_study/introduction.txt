4.1 Introduction: Introduce the tool or papers and its basic information
4.1.1 Introduction, design motivation, background knowledge, design, or architecture
4.1.2 Challenges encountered during development, solutions, and any trade-offs made
4.2 Implementation: Hands-on usage and in-depth understanding of the tool
4.2.1 Use the tool in practice and provide examples (write your own examples, do not copy directly from documentation) or integrate the tool into your development process or Final Project
4.2.2 Analyze the tool’s performance, design, architecture, etc.
4.3 Reflections: Personal thoughts after using the tool
4.3.1 Strengths and weaknesses of the paper or tool
4.3.2 Suggestions for improvement, such as additional features or areas for enhancement
4.3.3 Comparison with other tools—do you prefer this tool? Why or why not?
4.3.4 Will you adopt this tool in the future? Why or why not?
